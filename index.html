Stephen S. Krawczyk

WWII Veteran & Devoted Family Man

A Pearl Harbor survivor who was enlisted in the American United States Army Air Corps during World War II, Krawczyk has been lauded for his excellence during his military career and for his love and dedication to his family.

This biographical website aims to educate friends, family, and history buffs alike on the captivating life of Stephen Stanley Krawczyk.
